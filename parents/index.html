Is Your Child Coding After School?

Coding After School is a programming club for kindergarteners and first graders at Spicewood Elementary. Inspired by the success of the Hour of Code and based on the same educational software and curriculum from Code.org, Coding After School is a fun way for kids to learn to program a computer.

The club should motivate students to learn and practice programming on their own, and they will be sent home with additional resources for coding outside the club.
