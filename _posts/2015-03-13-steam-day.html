STEAM Day

  • 2nd and 3rd: rocket-launch
  • 4th and 5th: letter-zap

Come join us at Spicewood's Science, Technology, Engineering, Art, and Mathematics Day for fun learning to program using Scratch. STEAM Day gives students a chance to get some hands-on experience with science and technology by rotating through a variety of 30 minute sessions around the school.

We'll be setup in the library using our netbooks for paired programming. Though Scratch isn't the programming tool we'll be using for Coding After School, it is similar in spirit to the tools we'll be using at Code.org. Code.org serves as a more directed learning experience while Scratch is much more open-ended, a nice take-home complement to Coding After School.
