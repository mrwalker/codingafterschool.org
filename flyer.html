Kinder Coding Club

        Coding After School: Spring 2015

Does Your Kindergartener Like Computers?

Kinder Coding Club is a programming club for kindergarteners at Spicewood Elementary. Inspired by the success of the Hour of Code and based on the same educational software and curriculum from Code.org, Kinder Coding Club is a fun way for kids to learn to program a computer.

Curriculum

In Kinder Coding Club, we'll be studying Course 1 from Code.org, a course for early readers ages 4-6. We're going to start simple and work our way up to building our own game over the remainder of the spring semester.

The club should motivate students to learn and practice programming on their own, and they will be sent home with additional resources for coding outside the club.

Teachers

Kinder Coding Club will be taught by Julie Haney, Spicewood's instructional technology specialist, Kellie Keating, a kindergarten teacher, and Matt Walker, Max's dad.
